About Us

Café Story

Coz Cup Coffee was founded in 2010 by two passionate coffee enthusiasts, Miguel and Kier, in the heart of a vibrant neighborhood. The inspiration behind the café's establishment stemmed from their shared love for high-quality coffee and a desire to create a cozy space for the community.

Meet the Team

Kier

Owner

Bio: Kier, a dedicated coffee shop owner, infuses warmth and community into every cup at "Cozy Cup Coffee," where his commitment to quality brews and inviting ambiance creates a haven for coffee enthusiasts to savor the perfect blend.

Miguel

Owner

Bio: Miguel, a visionary coffee shop owner, orchestrates a sensory journey at "Cozy Cup Coffee," where his passion for ethically sourced beans and artisanal brewing transforms every visit into a delightful experience for patrons seeking a harmonious blend of flavor and ambiance.

Café's Mission

"At Coz Cup Coffee, our mission is to create a haven for coffee enthusiasts, where the rich aroma of freshly brewed beans meets the warmth of community. We strive to offer more than just a cup of coffee; we aim to provide a cozy and welcoming space that becomes an extension of your home, fostering connections, conversations, and a shared love for exceptional coffee."
